About the Candidate

Built for Service,
Proven in Leadership

Engr Okon Bassey is an Engineer, community champion, and dedicated family man who has spent years advocating for the ordinary citizen. Born and raised in the constituency, he understands the real challenges facing our people and has the vision and capacity to drive transformational change.

Integrity First A record of transparency, accountability, and ethical governance in every role held.
People-Centered Leadership Policy decisions rooted in community consultation and grassroots engagement.
Proven Track Record Visible, measurable impact in education, infrastructure, and youth empowerment.

Campaign Rally Draws Record Crowd at Unity Ground — Over 20,000 Supporters in Attendance

In an overwhelming show of solidarity, citizens from all twelve wards gathered...

In an overwhelming show of solidarity, citizens from all twelve wards gathered to affirm their commitment to the vision of a new dawn. Thousands filled the Unity Ground as supporters waved banners, cheered passionately, and listened attentively to speeches focused on development, empowerment, infrastructure, and youth inclusion. Community leaders described the rally as one of the largest political gatherings ever witnessed in the region.
